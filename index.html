Help

How to Use LinkRide

  • 1. Click on “Find a Ride” to find available rides shared by others.
  • 2. Use “Offer a Ride” if you want to offer your vehicle seat to others.
  • 3. Open “Map Your Route” to view suggested matches or plan trips.
  • 4. Use the Join button to create or log in to your account.
  • 5. Have thoughts or suggestions? Submit your review using our form.

1. Sign Up & Login

2. Find or Offer a Ride

3. Ride Safely Together

Rate, review, and ride responsibly with verified community riders.

About

Why LinkRide?

LinkRide isn't just a project — it’s a step toward better, smarter, and more connected daily travel. Designed for communities, students, and local commuters, LinkRide helps reduce costs, traffic, and environmental impact through ride sharing.

🌍 Eco-Friendly Travel

Cut down carbon emissions and save fuel by sharing rides instead of traveling solo.

🤝 Connect Locally

Find people headed your way — whether to college, work, or events — and share the journey.

💸 Save Smart

Spend less on transportation and enjoy smarter travel choices with fewer hassles.
